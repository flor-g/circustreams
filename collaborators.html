Featured Collaborator: Ruiling Yige Studio

Studio Overview

Based in Shenzhen, Ruiling focuses on ceramic restoration—including Kintsugi (金缮) and Juci (锔瓷)—as well as forging and engraving. The studio combines traditional methods with modern aesthetics and is recognized as part of the Nanshan intangible cultural heritage projects.

Two brands flow from this practice: Ruiling Yige Studio for hands-on repair and ceramic work, and “松间物外”(Pine & Elsewhere) for cultural products inspired by Chinese classical aesthetics.

Founder Profile – Yang Ruiling 杨瑞玲

Heritage bearer for Juci, Yang Ruiling is a ceramic restorer, artisan, and designer. She uses traditional techniques to restore and reinterpret ceramics.
